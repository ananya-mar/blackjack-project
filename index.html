Instructions:

Blackjack is a card game where the goal is to have your cards total as close to 21 as possible without going over.

- Each player is dealt two cards initially.
- Number cards are worth their face value; face cards are worth 10; Aces can be 1 or 11.
- Players can enter "y" (draw another card) or "n" (keep their total).
- If your total goes over 21, you lose.
- After the player finishes, the dealer reveals their cards and hits until they reach at least 17.
- The highest total not exceeding 21 wins.
